# New recipe template for process_recipe.py

Section: Breakfasts
Name: Example breakfast with eggs
Tags: quick, egg, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - 4 large eggs
    - 2 turkey bacon rashers
    - 1/2 avocado
    - 60 g cherry tomatoes
  Optional add-ons:
    - 1 slice GF sourdough (Dave)
    - 15 g goat's cheese (Mel)

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 4 large eggs
    - 2 turkey bacon rashers
    - 1/2 avocado
    - 60 g cherry tomatoes
    - 1 slice GF sourdough
  Mel:
    - 3 large eggs
    - 2 turkey bacon rashers
    - 1/2 avocado
    - 60 g cherry tomatoes
    - 15 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Heat a pan over medium heat and cook the turkey bacon until crispy.
  - Add eggs and cook gently until scrambled.
  - Serve with avocado and tomatoes, plus the optional add-ons per person.
