Chicken Parmesan Style Bake
comfort-food gf
Base ingredients
chicken breast
tomato sauce
goat's cheese
potatoes
Dave
220 g chicken
250 g potatoes
35 g goat's cheese
Mel
160 g chicken
150 g potatoes
25 g goat's cheese
Method
  1. Top chicken with tomato sauce and cheese.
  2. Bake until cooked.
  3. Serve with potatoes.
