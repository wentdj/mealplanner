Frittata Cups
batch prep high-protein breakfast-hack
Base ingredients
24 large eggs
300 g spinach
2 red peppers
200 g turkey mince
1 red onion
60 g goat's cheese
4 days prep
24 cups total
Method
  1. Use 2 muffin tins simultaneously in the oven for efficiency.
  2. Sauté vegetables and turkey mince.
  3. Crack eggs into large bowl and whisk.
  4. Distribute filling evenly across muffin cups.
  5. Clearly label Mel's cups (with goat's cheese) with toothpick or colour-coded foil.
  6. Bake both trays at 180°C for 17–18 min.
  7. Allow to cool fully on rack before stacking — moisture is the enemy.
  8. Fridge: 4 days in airtight containers.
  9. Freeze: up to 1 month.
  10. Reheat from frozen: 2 min microwave or 8 min oven at 170°C.
  11. Do not reheat more than once.
