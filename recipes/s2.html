Banana & peanut butter protein smoothie
5 minGF/DF
Dave
1 large banana
2 tbsp peanut butter
30 g vanilla pea/rice protein
250 ml goat's milk or almond milk
Handful ice
~480 kcal · 36g P · 46g C · 18g F
Mel
½ banana
1 tbsp peanut butter
25 g vanilla pea/rice protein
200 ml goat's milk or almond milk
Handful ice
~290 kcal · 27g P · 24g C · 10g F
Method
  1. Add all ingredients to a blender. Blitz until smooth. Best consumed within 20 minutes. Ideal post-workout when you need protein quickly and don't want to cook.
