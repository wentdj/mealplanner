Baked egg & vegetable frittata cups
Batch-friendlyEgg-heavyGF/DF
Base (makes 12 cups)
12 large eggs
150 g spinach
1 red pepper, diced
100 g turkey mince or diced turkey
½ red onion, finely diced
Salt, pepper, paprika, dried oregano
Extras
30 g goat's cheese (Mel's cups)
Olive oil spray
Dave
4 frittata cups
+ 1 slice GF sourdough
+ 1 banana or fruit
~510 kcal · 45g P · 32g C · 18g F
Mel
3 frittata cups
+ 15 g goat's cheese crumbled in cups
+ Side salad leaves
~310 kcal · 30g P · 8g C · 17g F
Method
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C. Spray a 12-hole muffin tin generously with olive oil.
  2. Sauté diced onion and pepper in a pan for 3–4 min. Add turkey mince and cook through (5 min). Wilt in spinach. Season well with salt, pepper, paprika, oregano.
  3. Divide the filling evenly among the 12 muffin holes. For Mel's 3 cups, add a pinch of goat's cheese to each.
  4. Crack 1 egg into each cup (or beat eggs first and pour — both work). Top with a little extra seasoning.
  5. Bake 15–18 min until set and lightly golden. Cool slightly before removing. Store in fridge up to 4 days or freeze.
