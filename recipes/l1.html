Herbed chicken & egg salad bowl
15 minEgg-heavyGF/DF
Base ingredients
Cooked chicken breast
Hard-boiled eggs
Mixed salad leaves
Cucumber
Cherry tomatoes
Olive oil, lemon juice, Dijon mustard
Fresh parsley or dill
Dave
180 g chicken breast, sliced
2 hard-boiled eggs, halved
80 g mixed leaves
½ cucumber, 80 g tomatoes
1 tbsp olive oil + lemon
Rice cakes (2) on side
~560 kcal · 62g P · 18g C · 24g F
Mel
130 g chicken breast, sliced
2 hard-boiled eggs, halved
80 g mixed leaves
½ cucumber, 60 g tomatoes
1 tsp olive oil + lemon
20 g goat's cheese crumbled
~390 kcal · 47g P · 8g C · 19g F
Method
  1. Boil eggs 8–9 min for hard yolks. Cool in cold water, peel and halve.
  2. Slice cooked chicken breast (use leftover batch-cooked or quickly pan-fry in olive oil with salt and herbs — 4–5 min per side).
  3. Whisk together 1 tbsp olive oil, juice of ½ lemon, a dot of Dijon mustard (check it's GF), salt and pepper.
  4. Arrange leaves, cucumber, tomatoes. Top with chicken and eggs. Drizzle dressing.
  5. Mel: scatter goat's cheese. Dave: serve with 2 rice cakes alongside.
