Turkey & avocado lettuce wraps
10 minGF/DF
Dave
100 g sliced turkey breast
½ avocado, mashed
4 large cos/iceberg lettuce leaves
Squeeze lemon, salt, pepper
~270 kcal · 30g P · 8g C · 13g F
Mel
80 g sliced turkey breast
¼ avocado, mashed
4 large cos/iceberg lettuce leaves
Squeeze lemon, salt, pepper
~190 kcal · 23g P · 5g C · 9g F
Method
  1. Mash avocado with lemon juice, salt and pepper. Spread inside lettuce leaves. Fill with turkey slices. Roll and eat immediately. Excellent afternoon snack or light pre-workout fuel.
