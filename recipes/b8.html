Egg and Sweet Potato Hash
egg batch-cook gf
Base ingredients
eggs
sweet potato
onions
peppers
Optional add-ons
extra egg (Dave)
goat's cheese (Mel)
Dave
4 eggs
250 g sweet potato
50 g onion
80 g peppers
1 extra egg
Mel
3 eggs
150 g sweet potato
50 g onion
80 g peppers
20 g goat's cheese
Method
  1. Roast sweet potato cubes.
  2. Fry onions and peppers.
  3. Add sweet potato and top with eggs.
  4. Cook until eggs are set.
