Breakfast Burrito Bowl
egg high-protein gf
Base ingredients
eggs
turkey mince
peppers
salsa
Optional add-ons
rice (Dave)
avocado (Mel)
Dave
4 eggs
120 g turkey mince
80 g peppers
50 g salsa
150 g cooked rice
Mel
3 eggs
90 g turkey mince
80 g peppers
50 g salsa
1/4 avocado
Method
  1. Cook turkey mince and peppers.
  2. Scramble eggs separately.
  3. Combine and top with salsa.
