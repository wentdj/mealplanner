Turkey Bacon Egg Scramble
quick egg high-protein gf
Base ingredients
eggs
turkey bacon
avocado
cherry tomatoes
Optional add-ons
GF sourdough (Dave)
goat's cheese (Mel)
Dave
4 large eggs
3 turkey bacon rashers
1/2 avocado
80 g cherry tomatoes
2 slices GF sourdough
Mel
3 large eggs
2 turkey bacon rashers
1/2 avocado
80 g cherry tomatoes
20 g goat's cheese
Method
  1. Cook turkey bacon until crisp.
  2. Scramble eggs in the same pan.
  3. Serve with avocado and tomatoes.
  4. Add optional extras per person.
