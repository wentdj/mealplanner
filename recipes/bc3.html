Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs
batch prep roasted marinated
Base ingredients
8 to 10 chicken thighs
3 courgettes
4 peppers
400 g cherry tomatoes
2 red onions
3 lemons
8 garlic cloves
generous olive oil
quinoa
4 days prep
8 thighs total
Method
  1. Combine lemon juice, olive oil, crushed garlic, and herbs for marinade.
  2. Marinate chicken overnight (Sunday morning, cook Sunday evening).
  3. Divide portions: Dave 2 thighs per day, Mel 1 thigh per day.
  4. Use two large roasting trays.
  5. Batch-roast vegetables separately so they don't go soggy.
  6. Roast at 200°C for 35-40 minutes until golden.
  7. Keep chicken portions in separate containers.
  8. Reheat chicken in oven 10 min at 180°C or briefly in microwave.
  9. Cook quinoa daily (15 min).
  10. Fridge: 3–4 days. Freeze: 2 months (without quinoa).
  11. Roasted veg fridge: 3 days max.
