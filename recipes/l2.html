Turkey & egg fried rice (cauliflower or rice)
20 minEgg-heavyGF/DF
Base ingredients
Cooked turkey mince or diced turkey
Eggs
Cooked white or basmati rice
Frozen peas and sweetcorn
Tamari (GF soy sauce)
Garlic, ginger, sesame oil
Spring onions
Mel's swap
Use cauliflower rice (pre-riced) for lower carbs, or use less rice
Dave
150 g cooked turkey
2 eggs (scrambled in pan)
180 g cooked rice
60 g peas & sweetcorn
2 tsp tamari, sesame oil
~590 kcal · 55g P · 60g C · 14g F
Mel
130 g cooked turkey
2 eggs (scrambled in pan)
80 g cooked rice + 100 g cauliflower rice
50 g peas & sweetcorn
2 tsp tamari, sesame oil
~410 kcal · 46g P · 32g C · 12g F
Method
  1. Heat a wok or large pan over high heat. Add a little sesame oil, fry garlic and ginger 30 seconds.
  2. Add turkey and stir-fry 3–4 min until slightly crispy at edges.
  3. Push turkey to the side. Crack eggs in, scramble quickly until just set, then mix through the turkey.
  4. Add rice (and cauliflower rice for Mel), frozen peas and corn. Stir-fry everything together on high heat 3–4 min.
  5. Season with tamari to taste. Finish with sliced spring onions and a final drizzle of sesame oil.
