Veggie Omelette Bowl
egg veggie gf
Base ingredients
eggs
spinach
mushrooms
peppers
Optional add-ons
cooked potatoes (Dave)
goat's cheese (Mel)
Dave
4 eggs
100 g spinach
80 g mushrooms
80 g peppers
200 g cooked potatoes
Mel
3 eggs
100 g spinach
80 g mushrooms
80 g peppers
25 g goat's cheese
Method
  1. Sauté vegetables.
  2. Add beaten eggs and cook as an omelette.
  3. Fold and serve with optional extras.
