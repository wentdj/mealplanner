Batch
Turkey & Black Bean Chilli
batch prep Sunday-meal
Base ingredients
1 kg turkey mince
4 tins black beans
2 tins chopped tomatoes
600 ml passata
4 peppers
2 onions
8 garlic cloves
400 ml GF chicken stock
Spices
cumin
chilli powder
paprika
4 days prep
8 portions total
Method
  1. Use the largest pot you own. Brown the mince.
  2. Add diced vegetables and garlic.
  3. Add tomatoes, passata, stock, and beans.
  4. Simmer 25-30 minutes. Takes same active time regardless of scale.
  5. Cool completely before portioning into 8 airtight containers.
  6. Cook rice separately each day for best texture (12 min).
  7. Freezes perfectly up to 3 months in individual portions.
  8. Defrost overnight in fridge. Fridge-life: 4 days.
