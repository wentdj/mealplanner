Spanish-style baked eggs (piperade)
Batch-friendlyEgg-heavyGF/DF
Base ingredients
Eggs
Chopped tomatoes (tin)
Mixed peppers
Red onion
Chorizo (GF-certified)
Garlic, smoked paprika, cumin
Olive oil, fresh parsley
Dave
3 eggs baked in the sauce
60 g chorizo
¾ tin chopped tomatoes
½ pepper, ¼ onion
2 slices GF bread to dip
~620 kcal · 42g P · 38g C · 30g F
Mel
2 eggs baked in the sauce
40 g chorizo
¾ tin chopped tomatoes
½ pepper, ¼ onion
No bread — extra salad leaves
~360 kcal · 26g P · 18g C · 22g F
Method
  1. Heat olive oil in an oven-proof frying pan or cast iron skillet. Fry chorizo 2 min until it releases its oil.
  2. Add diced onion and pepper. Cook 5–6 min until softened. Add garlic, paprika, cumin — stir 1 min.
  3. Pour in chopped tomatoes, season well. Simmer 8–10 min until sauce thickens.
  4. Make wells in the sauce and crack in eggs. Transfer pan to 180°C oven (or keep on low hob with lid). Cook until whites are set but yolks still runny — about 8–10 min.
  5. Scatter fresh parsley. Dave: serve with GF bread. Mel: serve with side salad.
