Turkey & black bean chilli with rice
Batch-friendlyGF/DF
Base ingredients (serves 4)
500 g turkey mince
2 tins black beans
1 tin chopped tomatoes
1 tin passata (300 ml)
2 peppers, onion, garlic
Cumin, smoked paprika, chilli powder, oregano
Chicken stock (GF), olive oil
Dave
Large bowl chilli (330 g)
160 g cooked basmati rice
½ avocado on top
Fresh coriander
~720 kcal · 55g P · 78g C · 20g F
Mel
Medium bowl chilli (250 g)
80 g cooked basmati rice
¼ avocado on top
Fresh coriander
~470 kcal · 38g P · 48g C · 14g F
Method
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot. Brown turkey mince over high heat, breaking up with a spoon, 5–6 min. Season as you go.
  2. Add diced onion, peppers and garlic. Cook 5 min until softened. Add all spices and stir 1 min until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, passata, stock, and drained black beans. Stir well. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer 25–30 min, stirring occasionally, until thick and rich.
  4. Check seasoning — adjust chilli heat to preference. Cool and portion for batch cooking.
  5. Serve with basmati rice, avocado, and coriander. The chilli improves overnight — ideal for batch prep.
