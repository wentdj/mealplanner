Thai Green Curry
batch prep curry coconut
Base ingredients
1.2 kg chicken breast
4 tins coconut milk
4 to 5 tbsp GF Thai green curry paste
2 courgettes
200 g mangetout
200 g baby corn
2 onions
garlic
ginger
rice
4 days prep
8 portions total
Method
  1. Cook in a large stockpot or casserole dish.
  2. Make 2 separate pots if your largest pan can't hold double batch.
  3. Sauté garlic and ginger, add curry paste and coconut milk.
  4. Bring to simmer, add chicken and vegetables.
  5. Simmer 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Store rice separately.
  7. The curry reheats beautifully with splash of water/coconut milk if thickened.
  8. Fridge: 3 days.
  9. Freezes extremely well — up to 3 months.
  10. Freeze in meal-sized zip-lock bags laid flat.
  11. Reheat from frozen on hob over low heat, stirring, adding splash of coconut milk.
