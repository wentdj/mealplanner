Section: Batch
Name: Turkey & Black Bean Chilli
Tags: batch, prep, Sunday-meal

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - 1 kg turkey mince
    - 4 tins black beans
    - 2 tins chopped tomatoes
    - 600 ml passata
    - 4 peppers
    - 2 onions
    - 8 garlic cloves
    - 400 ml GF chicken stock
  Spices:
    - cumin
    - chilli powder
    - paprika

Portions:
  4 days prep:
    - 8 portions total

Method:
  - Use the largest pot you own. Brown the mince.
  - Add diced vegetables and garlic.
  - Add tomatoes, passata, stock, and beans.
  - Simmer 25-30 minutes. Takes same active time regardless of scale.
  - Cool completely before portioning into 8 airtight containers.
  - Cook rice separately each day for best texture (12 min).
  - Freezes perfectly up to 3 months in individual portions.
  - Defrost overnight in fridge. Fridge-life: 4 days.
