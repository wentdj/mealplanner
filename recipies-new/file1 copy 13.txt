Section: Dinners
Name: Turkey Chilli
Tags: batch-cook, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - turkey mince
    - kidney beans
    - tomatoes
    - peppers
    - rice

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 220 g turkey mince
    - 250 g cooked rice
    - 150 g beans
  Mel:
    - 160 g turkey mince
    - 120 g cooked rice
    - 100 g beans

Method:
  - Brown turkey mince.
  - Add vegetables and tomatoes.
  - Simmer 30 minutes and serve with rice.
