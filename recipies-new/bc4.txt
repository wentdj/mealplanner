Section: Batch
Name: Thai Green Curry
Tags: batch, prep, curry, coconut

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - 1.2 kg chicken breast
    - 4 tins coconut milk
    - 4 to 5 tbsp GF Thai green curry paste
    - 2 courgettes
    - 200 g mangetout
    - 200 g baby corn
    - 2 onions
    - garlic
    - ginger
    - rice

Portions:
  4 days prep:
    - 8 portions total

Method:
  - Cook in a large stockpot or casserole dish.
  - Make 2 separate pots if your largest pan can't hold double batch.
  - Sauté garlic and ginger, add curry paste and coconut milk.
  - Bring to simmer, add chicken and vegetables.
  - Simmer 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  - Store rice separately.
  - The curry reheats beautifully with splash of water/coconut milk if thickened.
  - Fridge: 3 days.
  - Freezes extremely well — up to 3 months.
  - Freeze in meal-sized zip-lock bags laid flat.
  - Reheat from frozen on hob over low heat, stirring, adding splash of coconut milk.
