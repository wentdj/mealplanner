Section: Batch
Name: Frittata Cups
Tags: batch, prep, high-protein, breakfast-hack

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - 24 large eggs
    - 300 g spinach
    - 2 red peppers
    - 200 g turkey mince
    - 1 red onion
    - 60 g goat's cheese

Portions:
  4 days prep:
    - 24 cups total

Method:
  - Use 2 muffin tins simultaneously in the oven for efficiency.
  - Sauté vegetables and turkey mince.
  - Crack eggs into large bowl and whisk.
  - Distribute filling evenly across muffin cups.
  - Clearly label Mel's cups (with goat's cheese) with toothpick or colour-coded foil.
  - Bake both trays at 180°C for 17–18 min.
  - Allow to cool fully on rack before stacking — moisture is the enemy.
  - Fridge: 4 days in airtight containers.
  - Freeze: up to 1 month.
  - Reheat from frozen: 2 min microwave or 8 min oven at 170°C.
  - Do not reheat more than once.
