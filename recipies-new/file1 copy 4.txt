Section: Breakfasts
Name: Egg and Sweet Potato Hash
Tags: egg, batch-cook, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - eggs
    - sweet potato
    - onions
    - peppers
  Optional add-ons:
    - extra egg (Dave)
    - goat's cheese (Mel)

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 4 eggs
    - 250 g sweet potato
    - 50 g onion
    - 80 g peppers
    - 1 extra egg
  Mel:
    - 3 eggs
    - 150 g sweet potato
    - 50 g onion
    - 80 g peppers
    - 20 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Roast sweet potato cubes.
  - Fry onions and peppers.
  - Add sweet potato and top with eggs.
  - Cook until eggs are set.
