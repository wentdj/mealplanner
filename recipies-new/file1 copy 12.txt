Name: Chicken Fajita Bowls
Tags: fresh, family, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - chicken breast
    - peppers
    - onions
    - rice
    - salsa

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 220 g chicken
    - 250 g cooked rice
    - 200 g vegetables
  Mel:
    - 160 g chicken
    - 120 g cooked rice
    - 200 g vegetables

Method:
  - Cook chicken with fajita seasoning.
  - Fry peppers and onions.
  - Serve over rice with salsa.
