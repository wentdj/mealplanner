Section: Breakfasts
Name: Veggie Omelette Bowl
Tags: egg, veggie, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - eggs
    - spinach
    - mushrooms
    - peppers
  Optional add-ons:
    - cooked potatoes (Dave)
    - goat's cheese (Mel)

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 4 eggs
    - 100 g spinach
    - 80 g mushrooms
    - 80 g peppers
    - 200 g cooked potatoes
  Mel:
    - 3 eggs
    - 100 g spinach
    - 80 g mushrooms
    - 80 g peppers
    - 25 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Sauté vegetables.
  - Add beaten eggs and cook as an omelette.
  - Fold and serve with optional extras.
