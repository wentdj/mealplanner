Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - chicken breast

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 200 g chicken breast
    - 250 g cooked rice
    - 150 g vegetables
    - 10 ml olive oil
  Mel:
    - 150 g chicken breast
    - 120 g cooked rice
    - 150 g vegetables
    - 10 ml olive oil

Method:
  - Cook chicken and rice.
  - Slice vegetables.
  - Assemble bowls and drizzle with oil.
