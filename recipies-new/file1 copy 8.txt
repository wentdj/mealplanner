Section: Lunches
Name: Turkey Sweet Potato Meal Prep
Tags: batch-cook, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - turkey mince
    - sweet potato
    - broccoli

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 200 g turkey mince
    - 300 g sweet potato
    - 150 g broccoli
  Mel:
    - 150 g turkey mince
    - 180 g sweet potato
    - 150 g broccoli

Method:
  - Roast sweet potatoes.
  - Brown turkey mince.
  - Steam broccoli and serve.
