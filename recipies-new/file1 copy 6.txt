Section: Breakfasts
Name: Breakfast Burrito Bowl
Tags: egg, high-protein, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - eggs
    - turkey mince
    - peppers
    - salsa
  Optional add-ons:
    - rice (Dave)
    - avocado (Mel)

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 4 eggs
    - 120 g turkey mince
    - 80 g peppers
    - 50 g salsa
    - 150 g cooked rice
  Mel:
    - 3 eggs
    - 90 g turkey mince
    - 80 g peppers
    - 50 g salsa
    - 1/4 avocado

Method:
  - Cook turkey mince and peppers.
  - Scramble eggs separately.
  - Combine and top with salsa.
