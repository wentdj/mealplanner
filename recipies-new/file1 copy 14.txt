Section: Dinners
Name: Chicken Curry and Rice
Tags: fresh, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - chicken breast
    - coconut milk
    - curry spices
    - rice

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 220 g chicken
    - 250 g cooked rice
    - 100 ml coconut milk
  Mel:
    - 160 g chicken
    - 120 g cooked rice
    - 75 ml coconut milk

Method:
  - Cook chicken and spices.
  - Add coconut milk.
  - Simmer and serve over rice.
