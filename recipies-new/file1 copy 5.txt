Section: Breakfasts
Name: Goat Cheese Egg Muffins
Tags: meal-prep, egg, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - eggs
    - spinach
    - peppers
    - goat's cheese

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 5 eggs
    - 80 g spinach
    - 60 g peppers
    - 30 g goat's cheese
  Mel:
    - 3 eggs
    - 80 g spinach
    - 60 g peppers
    - 20 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Mix all ingredients.
  - Pour into muffin tray.
  - Bake at 180°C for 20 minutes.
