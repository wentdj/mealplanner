Section: Dinners
Name: Chicken Parmesan Style Bake
Tags: comfort-food, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - chicken breast
    - tomato sauce
    - goat's cheese
    - potatoes

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 220 g chicken
    - 250 g potatoes
    - 35 g goat's cheese
  Mel:
    - 160 g chicken
    - 150 g potatoes
    - 25 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Top chicken with tomato sauce and cheese.
  - Bake until cooked.
  - Serve with potatoes.
