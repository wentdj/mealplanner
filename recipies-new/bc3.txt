Section: Batch
Name: Lemon Herb Chicken Thighs
Tags: batch, prep, roasted, marinated

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - 8 to 10 chicken thighs
    - 3 courgettes
    - 4 peppers
    - 400 g cherry tomatoes
    - 2 red onions
    - 3 lemons
    - 8 garlic cloves
    - generous olive oil
    - quinoa

Portions:
  4 days prep:
    - 8 thighs total

Method:
  - Combine lemon juice, olive oil, crushed garlic, and herbs for marinade.
  - Marinate chicken overnight (Sunday morning, cook Sunday evening).
  - Divide portions: Dave 2 thighs per day, Mel 1 thigh per day.
  - Use two large roasting trays.
  - Batch-roast vegetables separately so they don't go soggy.
  - Roast at 200°C for 35-40 minutes until golden.
  - Keep chicken portions in separate containers.
  - Reheat chicken in oven 10 min at 180°C or briefly in microwave.
  - Cook quinoa daily (15 min).
  - Fridge: 3–4 days. Freeze: 2 months (without quinoa).
  - Roasted veg fridge: 3 days max.
