Section: Breakfasts
Name: Turkey Bacon Egg Scramble
Tags: quick, egg, high-protein, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - eggs
    - turkey bacon
    - avocado
    - cherry tomatoes
  Optional add-ons:
    - GF sourdough (Dave)
    - goat's cheese (Mel)

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 4 large eggs
    - 3 turkey bacon rashers
    - 1/2 avocado
    - 80 g cherry tomatoes
    - 2 slices GF sourdough
  Mel:
    - 3 large eggs
    - 2 turkey bacon rashers
    - 1/2 avocado
    - 80 g cherry tomatoes
    - 20 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Cook turkey bacon until crisp.
  - Scramble eggs in the same pan.
  - Serve with avocado and tomatoes.
  - Add optional extras per person.
