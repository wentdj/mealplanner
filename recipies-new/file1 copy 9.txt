Section: Lunches
Name: Chicken Egg Salad
Tags: quick, high-protein, gf

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - chicken breast
    - eggs
    - mixed leaves
    - cucumber
    - tomatoes

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 180 g chicken breast
    - 3 eggs
    - 200 g salad vegetables
  Mel:
    - 120 g chicken breast
    - 2 eggs
    - 200 g salad vegetables

Method:
  - Cook eggs and chicken.
  - Chop vegetables.
  - Toss together and serve.
