Section: Lunches
Name: Mediterranean Chicken Bowl
Tags: gf, high-protein

Ingredients:
  Base ingredients:
    - chicken breast
    - quinoa
    - cucumber
    - tomatoes
    - goat's cheese

Portions:
  Dave:
    - 200 g chicken
    - 200 g cooked quinoa
    - 150 g vegetables
    - 25 g goat's cheese
  Mel:
    - 150 g chicken
    - 120 g cooked quinoa
    - 150 g vegetables
    - 20 g goat's cheese

Method:
  - Cook chicken and quinoa.
  - Chop vegetables.
  - Combine and top with goat's cheese.
